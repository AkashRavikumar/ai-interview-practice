AI-Powered Interview Practice App

An idea to make interviews smarter and more effective

Current Problem

Interviewers often spend time speaking with candidates who are not well-prepared or not the right fit. This wastes valuable time and slows down the hiring process. Candidates may feel nervous or unsure, which can lead to poor performance even if they have the right skills.

The Solution

An AI-powered app where candidates can practise interview questions before their actual interview. The AI provides instant feedback on answers, checks communication skills, technical knowledge and confidence, and produces a summary report for HR and interviewers.

Benefits

  • Candidates: Become more confident, better prepared and perform well in real interviews.
  • Interviewers: Save time by focusing on stronger, better-prepared candidates.
  • Company: Speeds up recruitment, ensures fairness, and supports hiring at scale.

Roadmap

Phase 1 – Helper Tool

  • Let candidates practise with question sets relevant to the position applied for.
  • Central database stores questions from all interviewers by position and category.
  • Acts as a practice platform for candidates, simulating real company-style interviews.
  • Provides instant AI feedback on communication, clarity and competency fit.
  • Ensures candidates come better prepared, saving interviewers’ time and making discussions more meaningful.

Phase 2 – Insight Generator

  • Produces structured reports for HR and interviewers before the real interview.
  • Helps compare candidates more fairly and consistently.
  • Surfaces early insights into candidate strengths and gaps (e.g., technical vs. soft skills).

Phase 3 – Screening Optimisation

  • Evolves into a screening support tool, like CV sorting but for interview readiness.
  • Uses AI insights to prioritise candidates → best-fit candidates get interviewed first.
  • Saves time and accelerates hiring by reducing hours spent on weak fits.
  • Scalable across teams, roles and regions, standardising the hiring experience globally.
