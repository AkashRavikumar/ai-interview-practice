AI-Powered Interview Practice App

Helping candidates shine and saving time for interviewers

The Problem

Hiring processes often consume significant interviewer time. Many candidates arrive underprepared, which results in repetitive early-stage interviews that don’t reveal true fit.

  • Interviewers spend hours asking the same basic questions.
  • Strong candidates aren’t always prioritized in the lineup.
  • Candidates feel anxious and uncertain, which reduces performance.

The Solution

An AI-powered app where candidates practice interview questions before the real interview. The AI provides instant feedback on their answers, highlighting strengths and areas to improve. A structured report is shared with HR, helping them focus interviews on deeper discussions.

  • Candidates practice in a safe, low-pressure environment.
  • Interviewers get insights before the first conversation.
  • The company ensures a fairer, more consistent hiring process.

How It Works

  1. Candidate applies for a job and receives a unique invite code.
  2. They log in with their email and code to access the app.
  3. They practice answering a short set of realistic interview questions.
  4. The AI listens, evaluates, and provides instant feedback.
  5. A simple report is generated for HR to review before the actual interview.

Benefits

  • Candidates: Build confidence, reduce anxiety, and understand expectations before the real interview.
  • Interviewers: Save time by skipping basics and focusing on higher-value discussions.
  • The Company: Faster hiring, more consistent candidate evaluation, and better global scalability.
