AI-Powered Interview Practice App

An idea to make interviews smarter and more efficient

Current Problem

Interviewers spend a lot of time talking to candidates who may not be well-prepared or the best fit. This takes up valuable time and slows down hiring.

The Solution

An AI-powered app where candidates can practice interview questions before the real interview. The AI provides instant feedback on their answers and generates a summary report for HR and interviewers.

Benefits

  • Candidates: Get better prepared, more confident, and perform better in real interviews.
  • Interviewers: Save time by focusing on stronger, better-prepared candidates.
  • T&T: Speeds up hiring, ensures fairness, and scales globally.
