AI-Powered Interview Practice App

An idea to make interviews smarter and more efficient

The Problem

Interviewers spend a lot of time with candidates who may not be fully prepared or the best fit. This slows down hiring and reduces efficiency.

The Solution

An AI-powered app where candidates can practice interview questions before the real interview. The AI provides instant feedback on their answers and generates a summary report for HR and interviewers.

How It Works

  1. Candidate applies for a job and receives a unique invite code.
  2. They log in to the app with their email and code.
  3. They practice answering a short set of interview questions.
  4. The AI listens, evaluates, and gives instant feedback (strengths + areas to improve).
  5. A simple report is generated for HR to review before the real interview.
flowchart TD A[Candidate Applies] --> B[Gets Invite Code] B --> C[Logs into App] C --> D[Practices Interview Questions] D --> E[AI Analyzes & Gives Feedback] E --> F[Report Generated for HR] F --> G[HR Prepares for Interview]

Benefits

  • Candidates: Better prepared, more confident, and perform stronger in interviews.
  • Interviewers: Save time by focusing on the most prepared candidates.
  • T&T: Faster hiring, consistent process, and scalable globally.

Roadmap

  • Phase 1: Practice tool for candidates with instant feedback.
  • Phase 2: Reports and insights for HR to compare candidates fairly.
  • Phase 3: Screening tool to prioritize the best-fit candidates for early interviews.
