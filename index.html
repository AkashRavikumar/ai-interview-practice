AI-Powered Interview Practice App

An idea to make interviews smarter and more effective

Current Problem

Interviewers often spend time speaking with candidates who are not well-prepared or not the right fit. This wastes valuable time and slows down the hiring process. Candidates may feel nervous or unsure, which can lead to poor performance even if they have the right skills.

The Solution

An AI-powered app where candidates can practise interview questions before their actual interview. The AI provides instant feedback on answers, checks communication skills, technical knowledge and confidence, and produces a summary report for HR and interviewers.

Benefits

  • Candidates: Become more confident, better prepared and perform well in real interviews.
  • Interviewers: Save time by focusing on stronger, better-prepared candidates.
  • Company: Speeds up recruitment, ensures fairness, and supports hiring at scale.
