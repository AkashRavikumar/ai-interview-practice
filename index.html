AI-Powered Interview Practice App

An idea to make interviews smarter and more efficient

Current Problem

Interviewers spend a lot of time talking to candidates who may not be well-prepared or the best fit. This takes up valuable time and slows down hiring. Candidates often feel nervous or unprepared, leading to poor interview performance even if they have the skills.

The Solution

An AI-powered app where candidates can practice interview questions before the real interview. The AI provides instant feedback on their answers, evaluates communication clarity, technical accuracy, and confidence. It then generates a summary report for HR and interviewers.

Benefits

  • Candidates: Get better prepared, more confident, and perform better in real interviews.
  • Interviewers: Save time by focusing on stronger, better-prepared candidates.
  • Company: Speeds up hiring, ensures fairness, and scales globally.
